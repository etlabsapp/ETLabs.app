Lattice³

A true 3D crossword lattice. Rotate a cube-like grid and solve words along the X, Y, and Z axes—without losing your place.

Get notified Back to products

MVP focus

Understandable in 30 seconds.

The MVP proves three things: the puzzle is understandable, the interaction is smooth, and solving in 3D is satisfying—not frustrating.

  • One format: 4×4×4 lattice
  • Handcrafted pack: 10–25 puzzles
  • Two validation modes: Relaxed (default) + Assist
  • Local progress: auto-save puzzle state

Readability helpers

Orientation, maintained.

3D is only fun if you can keep track of where you are. Lattice³ is built around gentle helpers that reduce spatial friction.

  • Focus mode: highlight the active word, dim the rest
  • Slice view: isolate one layer at a time
  • Clues by axis: grouped as X / Y / Z
  • Auto-orient: tap a clue to rotate to a clear angle

Input

Fast entry, calm UI.

Tap a cell, choose an axis when needed, type. Letters advance through the active word; delete moves back. iPad hardware keyboards are supported.

  • Rotate: two-finger drag
  • Zoom: pinch
  • Select: tap
  • Hints: check/reveal letter or word
