How it works

Night planning → rest ritual → wake recovery → long-term reflection

Three loops connect in the app: you plan, you rest with structure, you recover after sleep or a nap, and you revisit history when you choose—not as an endless dashboard.

01

Night planning

Clock home (with optional SleepTight+ intelligence on the same surface), unified planner, cycle- or wake-target planning, bedtime suggestions, then alarm confirmation.

02

Rest ritual

Guided bedtime content for your chosen mode—prayer, affirmations, gratitude, or reflection—with time-of-day personalization, then active sleep or nap with Live Activities where supported.

03

Wake recovery

Wake confirmation, staged morning or nap check-ins, typed dreams and reflections, and local voice memos. Morning Score reveal and breakdown are SleepTight+.

04

Long-term reflection

History, archive browsing, text search, and analytics. Free users review typed dreams for the last 7 days. SleepTight+ unlocks DreamWrite: full archive, on-device transcription, and transcript-backed search.

SleepTight vs SleepTight+

Free core. Optional intelligence and DreamWrite.

Voice recording is free for everyone. DreamWrite (transcription, full typed archive beyond seven days, and transcript search) is part of SleepTight+, along with Morning Score, Sleep Debt, Ideal Bedtime Tonight, and the Weekly Sleep Report.

Premium boundary

  • Voice memos — record and save locally for free; transcription is SleepTight+.
  • Typed dreams — review the last 7 days free; full history beyond that is SleepTight+.
  • Transcripts & search — on-device DreamWrite transcription and transcript-backed archive search are SleepTight+ only.

Included without a subscription

  • Bedtime planning; cycle-based and wake-target planning
  • Alarm scheduling; nap planning
  • Guided bedtime ritual
  • Sleep wake confirmation; nap wake confirmation
  • Morning check-ins; nap check-ins
  • Typed dreams and reflections
  • Local voice memo recording
  • History and archive browsing
  • Free typed dream review for the last 7 days
  • Analytics browsing
  • Bedtime reminders
  • Live Activities

SleepTight+ (subscription)

  • Morning Score (with breakdown and framing)
  • Sleep Debt
  • Ideal Bedtime Tonight
  • Weekly Sleep Report
  • DreamWrite: full archive beyond 7 days
  • DreamWrite: searchable voice journaling (transcripts)
  • On-device transcription for dream and reflection memos
  • Transcript-backed history search
  • Premium paywall, purchase, and restore (Apple)

Privacy-first

Local-first, on your device

Sessions, voice memos, and DreamWrite transcripts stay on your iPhone. Transcription runs on device. There is no cloud sync of personal sleep or journal data in the shipped product. Deleting a session removes linked audio and transcript locally. Subscription and billing use Apple’s standard StoreKit flows.
