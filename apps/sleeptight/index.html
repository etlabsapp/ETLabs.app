How it works

Plan. Rest. Wake.

SleepTight guides you from onboarding and personalization through nightly planning, guided rituals, active rest, and morning recovery — with history, search, and analytics that get smarter over time.

01

Plan

Onboard once, set your preferences, then use the unified planner for sleep and naps with clear, finishable flows.

02

Rest

Move through a guided bedtime ritual, active sleep or nap screens, and Live Activities that keep your phone calm but informative.

03

Wake

Confirm wake, reflect with morning check-ins, and let SleepTight+ turn your history into Morning Score, Sleep Debt, and ideal bedtime suggestions.

SleepTight+ premium layer

Intelligence on top of a calm, free foundation.

The core SleepTight experience — planning, rituals, Live Activities, history, and basic analytics — is designed to feel complete on its own. SleepTight+ adds a premium intelligence layer that helps you understand patterns and make better decisions without turning sleep into a grind.

01

Morning Score & Sleep Debt

See a simple Morning Score, understand how rested you are, and track Sleep Debt so you can course-correct without obsessing over every data point.

02

Ideal Bedtime & Weekly Reports

Get an “Ideal Bedtime Tonight” suggestion and gentle Weekly Sleep Reports that frame what’s working — and what might need attention.

03

DreamWrite & searchable archive

Record dreams and reflections for free. Upgrade to SleepTight+ to unlock DreamWrite transcription, transcript-backed search, and a smarter personal archive.
