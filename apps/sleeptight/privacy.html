Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026

ETLabs (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates SleepTight. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the SleepTight app, or visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with SleepTight, we may collect the following:

a. Information you provide directly

If you contact us or otherwise submit information through the website, we may collect your email address and any information you include in your message.

b. App-related information

SleepTight may store certain app preferences, sleep planning information, check-ins, or related app content locally on your device in order to provide core app functionality.

c. Usage data

We do not run third-party analytics on the SleepTight app. For the ET Labs website, we may use limited aggregate analytics to understand traffic and improve the site.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • provide and improve SleepTight and related services,
  • respond to questions, requests, or support inquiries,
  • send product-related communications only if you explicitly request updates,
  • monitor and improve website and app performance, and
  • protect against abuse, spam, or misuse.

3. Email Communications

If you submit your email address through our website, we may use it to respond to your message and (only if you ask) send you updates about SleepTight or related product news. You may request removal from future communications at any time by contacting us at etlabs.app@gmail.com.

4. Data Storage

Some app data may be stored locally on your device to support SleepTight features. Website form submissions may be stored by our hosting or website service providers, such as Netlify, in order to process contact requests.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers that help us operate the website, host forms, analyze traffic, or support product functionality. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users.

6. Third-Party Services

SleepTight and the ETLabs website may rely on third-party services for hosting, analytics, email, social media links, or app distribution. Those services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no method of transmission, storage, or electronic security is guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

SleepTight is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Your Choices

You may:

  • choose whether to submit your email through the website,
  • contact us to request deletion of contact form information, and
  • stop using the app at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date shown at the top of this page.
