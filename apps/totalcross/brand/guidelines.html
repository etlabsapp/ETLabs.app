Total Cross — Guidelines

2026

The mark

Total Cross is a 5×5 crossword. TOTAL runs across, CROSS runs down, and they meet at a single shared O — always shown in Signal Red. Every other lettered cell is white with a black grid stroke. The mark reads as a logo first and a puzzle second.

Construction

Grid
5 × 5 cells, square.
Across
TOTAL — row 3, columns 1–5.
Down
CROSS — column 2, rows 1–5.
Pivot
Shared O at (row 3, col 2). Always Signal Red.
Stroke
1 unit when grid = 40 units. Min 1.5px on screen.
Letters
Archivo 800. Optical-centered. ~58% of cell height.
Clearspace
One full empty cell on every side of the grid.
Min size
Digital 24px tall · Print 12mm tall.
Clearspace = 1 cell

Color

Three colors. Don't add more.

Signal Red#D7321F · oklch(56% 0.20 28)RGB 215 50 31
Grid Ink#14110DRGB 20 17 13
Cell White#FFFFFFRGB 255 255 255

Paper #F3EFE9 is the off-white used for slides, decks, and stationery — not a logo color.

Type

Display & UI

Archivo 800

Archivo 600 / 700 · The geometric grotesk used inside the logo cells, in headlines, and on UI labels.

Editorial / accent

Fraunces 700

Fraunces italic for editorial flourish — clue numbers, pull quotes, headlines.

Mono / metadata

JetBrains Mono · for crumbs, captions, file paths, and anywhere the brand wants to feel like a puzzle clue. Tracked +0.14em uppercase for labels.

Voice

Tone

Crisp. Wry. A little self-amused. Confident enough to leave white space. Never breathless, never twee.

Lean on puzzle vocabulary — across, down, clue, fill, solve, pivot — without overusing it.

Naming

Always two words: Total Cross. Never "TotalCross" or "Total-Cross". Lowercased only inside URLs.

The shared red O is the brand's pivot. That's the noun.

Do · Don't

✓ DO

Keep one full cell of clearspace. Black grid, white cells, red O.

✗ DON'T

Don't place the light mark on a tinted background. Use the dark or reverse variant instead.

✗ DON'T

Don't stretch, skew, or rotate the grid. Cells must remain square.

✗ DON'T
CRTOTALSS

Don't reproduce below 24px tall on screen — the cells lose their stroke.

✗ DON'T

Don't apply gradients, shadows, glows, or other effects to the mark.

✗ DON'T
CRTOTALSS

Only the shared O is red. Never re-color other cells, even for emphasis.
