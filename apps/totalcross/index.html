How to play

Total Cross is a crossword where letters have values. Every word must add up to its target number.

  • A = 1, B = 2, C = 3 … Z = 26
  • Two themes are shown — one for Across words, one for Down words.
  • The target sum appears at the end of each word in the grid.
  • Crossing letters must satisfy both their Across and Down word.

Example — Theme: Big Cats, Target: 51

LION 12+9+15+14 = 50 ✗
PUMA 16+21+13+1 = 51 ✓
LYNX 12+25+14+24 = 75 ✗

      How it works

      A crossword with a twist.

      Every letter has a value — A is 1, B is 2, all the way to Z at 26. Two themes are shown: one for Across words, one for Down. The target sum at the end of each word is your only clue. Think of a word that fits the theme and hits the number.

      ∑

      Sum to win

      Every word must hit its exact target. The theme narrows the options — the math confirms the answer.

      ⊕

      Crossings count twice

      Shared letters must satisfy both their Across and Down word at once. That's where it gets interesting.

      ◑

      Three difficulties

      Easy pre-fills crossing letters. Medium gives you three reveals. Hard gives you nothing — just the grid and the numbers.
