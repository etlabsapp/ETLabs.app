How to play

Total Cross is a crossword where letters have values. Every word must add up to its target number.

  • A = 1, B = 2, C = 3 … Z = 26
  • Two themes are shown — one for Across words, one for Down words.
  • The target sum appears at the end of each word in the grid.
  • Crossing letters must satisfy both their Across and Down word.
  • Stuck? Use a Hint to reveal a letter — each one adds 60 seconds to your time.

Example — Theme: Big Cats, Target: 51

LION 12+9+15+14 = 50 ✗
PUMA 16+21+13+1 = 51 ✓
LYNX 12+25+14+24 = 75 ✗
